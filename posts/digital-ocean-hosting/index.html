Why Digital Ocean is a great hosting platform for your SaaS product

Sep 12, 2022

When you're building a SaaS product, it's important to choose a hosting platform that is reliable, scalable, and easy to use. One option that meets all of those criteria is Digital Ocean. In this blog post, we'll take a closer look at what Digital Ocean has to offer and why it's such a great choice for hosting your SaaS product.

What is Digital Ocean?

Digital Ocean is a cloud computing platform that offers virtual private servers, or “droplets,” as they’re called. Droplets are typically used to host web applications, and they offer several benefits over traditional shared hosting environments.

For one, droplets are much more configurable than traditional shared hosting environments. This means that you can tailor your droplet to the specific needs of your application. For example, if you’re running a database-driven web application, you can choose to allocate more RAM and CPU resources to your droplet than if you were just running a simple static website.

Another benefit of Digital Ocean droplets is that they’re extremely scalable. If you start to see an increase in traffic to your web application, you can simply add more resources to your droplet (e.g., additional RAM and CPU) without having to migrate to a new server. This vertical scaling is not possible with traditional shared hosting environments.

Finally, droplets are very easy to use and manage. The Digital Ocean control panel makes it simple to create and destroy droplets, add or remove resources from existing droplets, snapshot droplets for easy backups, and more. In addition, the platform offers a wide range of tutorials and guides that can help you get started with using droplets for your web application.

Pricing

One of the most attractive features of Digital Ocean is its pricing structure. Droplets start at just $5 per month for the most basic package (which includes 1 CPU core, 1 GB of RAM, 25 GB of SSD storage, and 1 TB of bandwidth). And if you need more resources, simply upgrade your package—additional CPU cores can be had for as little as $10 per month, while additional RAM starts at just $15 per month.

In short, Digital Ocean provides an extremely cost-effective way to host your web application—even if your application happens to be popular and sees a lot of traffic!

Conclusion

Digital Ocean is a great option for hosting your SaaS product due to its reliability, scalability, and ease of use. In addition, the platform offers an attractive pricing structure that makes it affordable for even the most popular web applications.
