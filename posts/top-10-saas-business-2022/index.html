Zendesk

Zendesk is a customer service software company that offers help desk and CRM software. The company was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in San Francisco, California. Zendesk has over 100,000 customers and more than 500 employees. What started as a startup has grown to one of the top SaaS companies in the world.

Zendesk’s customer service software allows businesses to manage customer interactions across multiple channels including email, phone, chat, social media, and support forums. The software also includes features such as ticket management, reporting and analytics, customer profile management, and knowledge base creation and management.

In addition to its customer service software, Zendesk also offers a suite of CRM tools that allow businesses to track and analyze customer data. The CRM tools include features such as contact management, sales pipeline management, lead tracking, and opportunity tracking.

Zendesk has received numerous awards over the years, including being named one of the fastest growing companies in Silicon Valley by the Silicon Valley Business Journal in 2016 and one of the best places to work by Glassdoor in 2017.

Oracle

Oracle is a widely used database management system. It was first released in 1977 and has since become one of the most popular database management systems in use today. Oracle is known for its reliability, scalability, and security. It is also a fairly expensive system to license, making it a choice for larger businesses and organisations.

Oracle is a proprietary software system, meaning that the source code is not publicly available. This has led to a number of open source alternatives such as MySQL, MariaDB, and PostgreSQL. While these alternatives are not as feature-rich as Oracle, they are considerably less expensive and can be adequate for many small to medium-sized businesses.

Workday

Workday is a cloud-based enterprise resource planning (ERP) system for small to medium businesses. It offers a range of features, including payroll, HR, time and attendance tracking, invoicing, and purchase order management. Workday also integrates with popular third-party apps, such as Salesforce and QuickBooks.

One of the benefits of using Workday is that it’s a subscription service. That means businesses only pay for the features they need and can add or remove users as their needs change. Another benefit is that updates and new features are released on a regular basis, so businesses always have access to the latest technology.

Workday is used by over 1,000 companies worldwide, including Coca-Cola, PepsiCo, and Ford Motor Company. It’s been named one of the best cloud-based ERP systems by Gartner and Forrester.

Hubspot

HubSpot is a digital marketing and sales platform that helps businesses grow inbound leads, convert more of those leads into customers, and optimize their marketing and sales efforts. Launched in 2006, HubSpot now has over 25,000 users in more than 90 countries.

The HubSpot platform includes a suite of tools to help businesses grow their website traffic, convert leads into customers, and measure the impact of their marketing and sales efforts. The platform includes:

Inbound Marketing: Tools to help businesses increase website traffic through search engine optimization (SEO), content creation and distribution,social media marketing, and lead generation forms.

Conversion Tools: Tools to help businesses convert more leads into customers through lead capture forms, lead nurturing emails, automatic lead scoring and grading, and integrations with CRM systems.

Marketing Analytics: Tools to help businesses measure the impact of their marketing and sales efforts through website analytics, email marketing metrics, social media analytics, and closed-loop reporting.

The HubSpot platform is free to use for small businesses with up to 1,000 contacts. Larger businesses can choose from a range of paid subscription plans starting at $200/month.

App Annie

App Annie is a mobile app analytics and market intelligence platform. It offers both free and paid subscription plans in a software as a service model.

The company was founded in 2010 by Olivier Bernard and Bertrand Serlet, who were both previously employed at Apple. App Annie has since received funding from several notable investors, including Sequoia Capital, Index Ventures, and Institutional Venture Partners.

The company’s headquarters are located in San Francisco, California. In addition to its main office, App Annie also has offices in Beijing, China and Tokyo, Japan.

App Annie offers a variety of services to its customers, including app ranking, trend analysis, market data, and consumer insights. The company also maintains a database of over 1 million apps and provides detailed information on app downloads, user demographics, spending patterns, and other data points.

In 2015, App Annie was ranked #5 on the Inc. 500 list of the fastest-growing private companies in America. The company’s annual revenue had grown by over 2,000% between 2011 and 2015.

Adobe

Adobe is an international software company that develops software for creating and managing documents, photos, and other digital content. Adobe was founded in 1982 by John Warnock and Charles Geschke, who both worked at the Xerox Palo Alto Research Center (PARC). The company’s first product was a desktop publishing program called Adobe Illustrator.

Today, Adobe is best known for its Photoshop and Acrobat software programs. Photoshop is a widely used image editor that allows users to create and edit photos, logos, and other graphic designs. Long a standalone product, Photoshop was converted to a SaaS product. Acrobat is a program used to create and edit PDF files.

Adobe also offers a number of other software products, including Premiere Pro (a video editing program), After Effects (a program used for creating motion graphics), InDesign (a program for creating print layouts), and Dreamweaver (a web design program).

In recent years, Adobe has shifted its focus to developing cloud-based software programs. These programs are available online and can be accessed from any device with an internet connection. Some of Adobe’s most popular cloud-based programs include Photoshop Elements (a simplified version of Photoshop), Premiere Rush (a video editing program designed for beginners), and After Effects CC (the latest version of After Effects).

Adobe is a publicly traded company and is listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange under the symbol ADBE. As of 2019, the company has a market capitalization of $129 billion.

Sprinklr

Sprinklr is a social media management platform that helps businesses manage their social media accounts more effectively. The company was founded in 2009 by Ragy Thomas, and has since grown to become one of the leading saas providers in the world.

Sprinklr’s platform allows businesses to connect with their customers on social media channels such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Instagram. The platform also provides users with a suite of tools to help them better understand and engage with their customers. These tools include customer segmentation, social listening, and content management.

Since its inception, Sprinklr has raised over $240 million in funding and has acquired several other companies, including Dachis Group and Socialdotcom. In addition to its headquarters in New York City, Sprinklr also has offices in London, Dubai, Bangalore, and São Paulo.

The company has been praised by several industry analysts for its innovative platform and its commitment to customer satisfaction. In 2016, Sprinklr was named a Leader in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for Social Media Management Solutions.

Docusign

Docusign is an electronic signature software that allows you to sign contracts and other documents electronically. It’s a handy tool for business owners and professionals who need to execute contracts quickly and without having to print, sign, and scan the documents. Docusign is also secure and compliant with industry regulations, so you can rest assured that your sensitive information is protected.

To use Docusign, you’ll need to create an account. Once you’re logged in, you can add your signature to any document by clicking the “Sign” button. You can also send documents for signature by others, and they can sign them electronically too. Docusign also lets you track the status of documents, so you always know what’s been signed and what still needs to be done.

If you’re looking for a fast, secure, and easy-to-use electronic signature solution, then Docusign is a great choice. With Docusign, you can get your work done quickly and efficiently without having to worry about the security of your information.
